About Us

At Timber and Touch, we aim to transform how people design and create furniture. Our platform uses cutting-edge augmented reality (AR) technology to allow users to visualize their designs in real-time. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast, professional designer, or furniture manufacturer, we provide the tools to customize and bring your vision to life. Our mission is to make furniture design accessible, engaging, and seamless.

Features

  • AR Visualization: View your designs in real-time with AR technology.
  • Customization: Customize furniture size, shape, and material.
  • User-Friendly: Intuitive interface for both beginners and professionals.
